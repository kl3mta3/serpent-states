🌿 Did You Know?

Fascinating Snake Facts

These incredible creatures have been around for over 100 million years.

🌡️

Cold-Blooded Marvels

Snakes are ectothermic — they regulate body temperature using their environment. That's why you'll often find them sunbathing on warm rocks!

👁️

No Eyelids

Snakes can't blink! They have a transparent scale called a "spectacle" or "brille" that protects their eyes at all times.

🦴

Flexible Jaws

A snake's lower jaw isn't fused — it's connected by elastic ligaments, allowing them to swallow prey much larger than their head.

📏

200-400 Vertebrae

Snakes have an incredibly flexible spine with 200 to 400 vertebrae, each with a pair of ribs. Humans have only 33!

👃

Smell with Their Tongue

A snake's forked tongue collects chemical particles from the air and delivers them to the Jacobson's organ for "smelling."

🐣

Not All Lay Eggs

About 30% of snake species give live birth! Rattlesnakes, boas, and garter snakes are all viviparous or ovoviviparous.
